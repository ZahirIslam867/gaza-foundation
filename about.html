About MAP

Medical Aid
for Palestinians

MAP works for the health and dignity of Palestinians living under occupation and as refugees. They deliver medical supplies, support hospitals, train healthcare workers, and provide emergency response in Gaza, the West Bank, and Palestinian refugee camps across the region.
